About Us

We Produce Organic Food For Your Family

AVJ Farm is a sustainable aquaponics farm located in the lush green village of Thodupuzha, Kerala. We combine modern aquaponic methods with traditional values to produce fresh fish and vegetables in harmony with nature. Our system uses 90% less water, no chemicals, and produces clean, high-nutrient food that supports both our community and our environment.

100% Organic

We grow everything the natural way — without synthetic pesticides, harmful fertilizers, or GMO seeds. At AVJ Farm, “organic” isn't just a label — it's our commitment to health, sustainability, and clean food. Every leaf and fish you get from us is safe, nutritious, and responsibly grown.

Award Winning

AVJ Farm has been recognized for its pioneering work in sustainable aquaponics and eco-friendly agriculture. Our innovative farming methods, commitment to chemical-free produce, and community-focused practices have earned us accolades at both local and regional levels — making us a trusted name in clean, conscious farming.

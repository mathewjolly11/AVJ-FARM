About AVJ Farm

We Grow with Nature — Aquaponically

AVJ Farm is a pioneering aquaponics farm located in Thodupuzha, Kerala. We grow vegetables and fish together in a self-sustaining ecosystem, producing nutritious food with 90% less water and zero chemicals.

Our mission is to offer a clean, eco-friendly, and modern alternative to conventional farming — bringing you truly fresh food, every day.
